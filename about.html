About Us

We are a little town called Cape Warmmis. Cape Warmmis is located on the northern seashore. This little town is home to a vibrant culture. Come visit us!

Our Fall Fiesta is located in the center of town across from Town Hall.

The Corn Maze and Tractor rides are loacted west of the parking lot.

Farmers Market for everyone. If you want food or clothing look no further. If you want games, jewlery, weapons, classes on all the lattest trends, and more! You can find them all here in our little Farmers Market.

Rollar Coasters for the thrill seekers!

Where

We are located in the northern US and you can find us on any map just look up Cape Warmmis. Our little town has a crisp air that reminds you of the perfect fall. We are in the middle of an Oak woods.

Live Performances! Local bands and singers alike flock to our town before they become famous to meet teir future fanbase. Britney Spears, Arinana Grande, Imagine Dragons, BTS, and more!

History

The Fall Festival begain in the early 1920s as a small comumunity get together during the great depresion. The comunity came together, there was dancing, music, games, and of course food. Everyone chipped in to help raise the money to make a cake for the whole town to share. The comunity would come together to boost moral and it created a tight nit comunity.

Later our little comunity grew and developed a better festival like the ones we have now. Local musicians, bands, small buisnesses, and even some famous celebrities all come to our little town for the Fall Fiesta.

Our little Fall Fiesta as we call it has now become a major event attracting thousands of visitors annually. With the modern advancments of todays technology we now how many attractions.

We now have a culturally rich comunity.
